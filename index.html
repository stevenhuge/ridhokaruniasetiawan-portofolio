About Me

I am a dedicated and certified Junior Web Developer, originally from Karanganyar Regency and a graduate of SMK Negeri 2 Surakarta, where I majored in Software Engineering. My passion for technology was sparked in vocational school, leading me to pursue comprehensive training programs and achieve key industry certifications from BNSP.

My professional experience includes a formative internship at Gadjah Mada University's Vocational School, where I contributed as both an Android and Frontend Web Developer, gaining valuable hands-on experience in a dynamic academic environment.

Currently, I am advancing my expertise by pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Information Systems at Amikom University Yogyakarta. My academic pursuits are complemented by a deep dive into game development, where I am mastering new programming languages such as C++. As a creator, I also develop educational IT-themed games using Roblox Studio.

Driven by an entrepreneurial spirit and a desire to create impactful solutions, I have founded FoodLink, a business product designed to combat food waste in Indonesia. My overarching career goal is to continue to innovate at the intersection of technology and business, leveraging my skills to solve meaningful real-world challenges.
